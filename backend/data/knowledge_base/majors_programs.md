# Chico State Majors and Programs

Source: https://www.csuchico.edu/academics/majors-programs/index.shtml

## Overview

California State University, Chico offers more than 300 academic options across seven colleges focused on subjects from agriculture and business to science and the arts. Programs include undergraduate majors, undergraduate minors, graduate degrees, and professional certifications/credentials.

## Degree Types Available

- Undergraduate Majors
- Undergraduate Minors
- Graduate Degrees
- Credentials (teaching and professional)

## Delivery Formats

- On Campus
- Online
- Hybrid

## Seven Colleges at Chico State

1. College of Agriculture
2. College of Behavioral and Social Sciences
3. College of Business
4. College of Communication and Education
5. College of Engineering, Computer Science, and Construction Management
6. College of Humanities and Fine Arts
7. College of Natural Sciences

## Why Chico State Academics

- Regularly named among U.S. News & World Report's Top Public Universities in the West
- Historic campus within walking and biking distance of Chico's charming, tree-lined downtown
- Graduates go on to become leaders in their careers and communities
- More affordable than almost any other four-year college
- Students graduate with some of the lowest debt in the country
