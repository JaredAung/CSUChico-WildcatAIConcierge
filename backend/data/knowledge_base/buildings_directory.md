# Campus Buildings Directory - CSU Chico

Source: Printable Campus Map (March 2025), https://www.csuchico.edu/_assets/documents/office/admissions/printable-campus-map.pdf

## Campus Information

- **Information Center:** 530-898-4636, info@csuchico.edu
- **University Police:** 530-898-5555 (24-hour, non-emergency), 911 (emergency)
- **Campus Office Hours:** Academic Year 8 a.m.–5 p.m. (PST), Monday–Friday; Summer 7 a.m.–5:30 p.m. (PST), Monday–Thursday, Closed Fridays
- **Address:** 400 West First Street, Chico, CA 95929

## Campus Streets

The campus is bounded by these streets:
- **North:** West Sacramento Avenue, La Vista Way
- **South:** West First Street, First Street Promenade
- **East:** Normal Avenue, Salem Street, Broadway, Esplanade
- **West:** Warner Street, Cedar Street, Stadium Way

Key internal streets:
- West Second Street (runs east-west through campus center)
- West Third Street
- Chestnut Street (runs north-south)
