## What Are Basic Needs?

- **Food Security:** Access to nutritious, sufficient, and culturally appropriate food for all individuals
- **Housing Stability:** Access to secure, stable, and affordable housing options to prevent homelessness and housing insecurity
- **Financial Stability and Wellness:** Ability to manage expenses, build savings, and achieve financial independence and well-being
- **Healthcare Access:** Access to affordable, accessible, and quality healthcare services for both physical and mental health needs
- **Childcare Accessibility:** Access to affordable, reliable, and high-quality childcare to support families and working parents
