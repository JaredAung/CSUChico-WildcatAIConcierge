## Details

The Rawbar serves much more than just sushi, including appetizers, soups & salads, rice bowls, and tempura. We offer a wide range of sakes, as well as our own signature saketini drinks, cocktails, traditional teas, beer, wine and full bar service. Our sushi includes traditional and contemporary sashimi, nigiri, and rolls. We also have a wide variety of hot appetizers, entrees, salads and sushi platters available for catering.
