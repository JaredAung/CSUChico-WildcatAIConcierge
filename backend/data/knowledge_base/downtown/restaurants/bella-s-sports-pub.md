## Details

Welcome to Bella's Sports Pub

 Purchased on New Year's Eve in 2008 and originally nestled at 134 Broadway St, Bella's Sports Pub has evolved into a staple of downtown Chico. Now proudly situated at 231 Main St since March 14, 2020, we've reimagined our classic dive into a vibrant hub for all things sports. Our inviting atmosphere, complete with multiple high-definition TVs and a spacious patio bar, ensures the ultimate game-day experience for every fan. Whether you're here to enjoy our mouth-watering wings, decadent tacos, or delicious smash burgers, you'll find the perfect pairing for cheering on your favorite team. And of course, no visit is complete without indulging in our iconic Soccer Mom Shot—a fan-favorite that's both refreshing and spirited, bringing regulars and newcomers together in a shared love for good times and great sports. So come on down to Bella's Sports Pub, where every game feels like a home game!
