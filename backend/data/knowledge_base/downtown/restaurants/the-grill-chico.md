## Details

Our mission is to create a warm, inviting, and cozy dining experience right in the heart of Downtown Chico. We take pride in serving unique, comforting classics made in-house with the freshest, highest-quality ingredients. Whether you're stopping by for a drink and a meal, enjoying a family dinner, or celebrating a private event, we're delighted to be part of your special moments.

 As a locally owned and operated family business, proudly part of the Hotel Diamond family and conveniently located on the first floor, we invite you to slow down, relax, and truly enjoy the company of those around you. Surround yourself with beautifully preserved architectural elements that tell the story of our building's rich history, all while savoring delicious food and drinks.

 Thank you for making us a part of your family. Welcome to our home, where you're always among friends.
