## Details

Experience authentic New York-style pizza in the heart of Downtown Chico at Bodega Slice Shop.

 Known for their perfectly crafted slices and whole pies, Bodega offers daily favorites alongside creative rotating specials. The casual, counter-service atmosphere captures the essence of a classic NYC pizzeria, making it a perfect stop for a quick lunch or family dinner.
