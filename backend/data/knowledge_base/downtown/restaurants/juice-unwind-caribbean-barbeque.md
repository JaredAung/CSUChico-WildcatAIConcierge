## Details

Juice Unwind Caribbean Barbecue in Downtown Chico is a standout destination for bold, flavorful Caribbean cuisine and warm, welcoming service. Guests can't get enough of the tender, juicy meats like jerk chicken, brisket, oxtail, and ribs, each dish packed with authentic island flavor and cooked to perfection. Portion sizes are generous, often enough to share or take home for later, making it a great value. The sides, including mac and cheese, rice and beans, collard greens, and Jamaican patties, perfectly complement the main dishes and round out a satisfying meal. The staff is consistently praised for their friendliness and efficiency, and the vibrant, casual setting with indoor and outdoor seating makes for a relaxed, enjoyable dining experience. Whether you're a fan of classic barbecue or looking to explore Caribbean flavors, Juice Unwind delivers a memorable and delicious experience every time.
