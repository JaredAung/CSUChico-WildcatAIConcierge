## Details

Located inside The Garden Walk in Downtown Chico.

 Boba Tealicious is the ultimate destination for boba lovers, offering an extensive selection of teas, smoothies, milk teas, fusion teas, and slushies. Known for their unique and delicious flavor combinations, like the crowd favorite Blueberry and Honeydew fusion with classic boba, it's no wonder customers rave about their wide range of options. Whether you're in the mood for a creamy Thai tea, a fragrant Taro+Lavender combo, or a refreshing fusion of two flavors, Boba Tealicious lets you customize your drink to perfection.

 Customers also love the quality of the boba itself—cooked just right for that perfect chewy texture—and the balanced sweetness of their beverages. The welcoming, cozy atmosphere and excellent customer service add to the experience, making it a go-to spot in Chico. While tucked away in a small mall, those in the know appreciate the clean, inviting vibe and are always coming back for more. If you're looking for a boba experience that's as delicious as it is memorable, Boba Tealicious is the place to be!
