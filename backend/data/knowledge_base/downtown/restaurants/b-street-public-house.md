About Us

 B Street Public House is a gastropub that serves New American fare in a rustic-chic location that features spacious patio seating, a full bar, and a popular weekend brunch.

 "B Street Public House is a dream creation, a coffee bar that over the course of the day morphs from a spot for brunch and lunch to a dinner house and eventually to a late-night music scene." ~Los Angeles Times
