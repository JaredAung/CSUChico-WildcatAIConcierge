About Broadway Heights California Cuisine

 Broadway Heights is a beloved dining icon in the heart of beautiful downtown Chico!

 A tasty option for students or downtown business lunches alike, Broadway Heights has created a legacy of consistently delicious sandwiches, soups, salads and more, focusing on house-made dishes, all from scratch. And, we're introducing new entrees and dinner items. Our baked focaccia sandwiches are Chico-famous!

 Our constant goal is to continue this tradition, and refine the downtown dining experience. We think quality dining in Chico should be comfortable, welcoming and inviting without sacrificing quality ingredients or flavor.

 We also love to cater! The restaurant, located in the beautiful and historic Phoenix Building in downtown Chico, is available for private parties up to 100 people (room rental fees may apply), and includes all linens, dishware, flatware, glasses, buffet or full table service.

 Off-site catering is also available. We can come to you with a range of services, from simply dropping off food for a meeting to providing a fully staffed banquet.

 All food is prepared with the highest quality ingredients to create a fresh and healthy blend that is sure to delight even the most discriminating palate. Broadway Heights is consistently voted "Best in Chico."
