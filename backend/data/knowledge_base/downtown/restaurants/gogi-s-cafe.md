## Details

Gogi's Cafe is a family-run North Indian Restaurant located in the heart of Downtown Chico in Northern California's Central Valley.  It is created and run by a husband & wife team of certified chefs from New Delhi, India who prepare and serve gourmet menu options from scratch every morning, or on order, with authentic herbs and spices.
