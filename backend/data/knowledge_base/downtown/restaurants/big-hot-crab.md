## Details

Have a hands-on meal.

 Big Hot Crab is a family-owned restaurant inspired by the Louisiana-style seafood boil, where heaps of delicious shellfish are cooked whole and served on the table for an indulgent, hands-on meal. Our mission has been to share this way of enjoying seafood, and since opening in 2015, we've been proudly serving seafood boils with our own twist on traditional Cajun flavors.
