 ARTober FEST 2025 Event Guide

 Overview

 The ARTober FEST celebration slated for October seeks to merge artistic expression with community engagement, creating a dynamic gathering that celebrates the rich cultural tapestry of Chico. Through partnerships with the Museum of Northern California Art (MONCA), Chico Art Center, Chico Arts Commission, Chico Arts & Culture Foundation, Chico Visual Arts Alliance, local educational institutions such as Butte College and Chico State, and a diverse array of local artists and organizations, ARTober FEST features a wide range of activities including live performances, art exhibitions, and workshops.

 Signature Events

- October 10 – Art & Wine Walk (5:00 – 8:00 PM): Enjoy the Downtown Chico Business Association Annual Art & Wine Walk! Since 2015 this event has showcased local artists, regional wine, beer, and cider tastings, and live music throughout Downtown Chico. Purchase your tickets today for only $20!

- October 11 – Harvest Sidewalk Sale (10:00 AM – 5:00 PM): Rake in the savings and celebrate the changing of the season! The Harvest Sidewalk Sale is the perfect opportunity to pick up some incredible deals and get a jump-start on holiday shopping.

- October 30 – Treat Street (2:00 – 5:00 PM): Join us for a fun evening of trick-or-treating, live music and photo opportunities with Chico PD and Fire!

 All Month Long

- October 1–31 – ARTober FEST Month Long Art Walk: Stroll through Downtown Chico to view the work of local artists in participating downtown businesses!

- October 1–31 (Fridays & Saturdays) – Chico Haunted Tours: Born from a group of friends with a love for ghost stories and local lore, Chico Haunted Tours takes you deep into the city's spine-tingling past. Every step is steeped in thoroughly researched history, and every ticket helps restore and preserve the legendary El Rey Theatre - a landmark as beautiful as it is haunted. Visit ChicoHauntedTours.com for more info.

- October 1–31 – Nexo Entre Raices at Janet Turner Print Museum: The Turner is excited to host Nexo Entre Raices, a portfolio bringing together 33 contemporary artists living and working on either side of the US-Mexico border. Organized by Marco Sanchez at Taller La Espina, this compelling print portfolio explores how our cultural roots shape and define us. Don't miss the Artist Talk & Opening Reception on Thursday, Oct. 2 from 5:30–7:30 PM in the Performing Arts Center (PAC) 134 at CSU Chico.
