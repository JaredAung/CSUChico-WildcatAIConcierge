 The Thursday Night Market transforms Downtown Chico into a vibrant, open-air celebration of community each week from April through September. With over 200 vendors, the market brings together local farmers, makers, food vendors, nonprofits, and small businesses for an experience that is as dynamic as it is welcoming.

 Stroll through downtown and discover fresh, locally grown produce, handmade goods, and unique finds while enjoying a lively lineup of musicians, performers, and entertainers. Each month introduces a new themed experience, from health and wellness to outdoor adventure, cultural celebration, nonprofit engagement, weddings, and even pets, giving every visit something new to explore.

 Families can enjoy expanded kids' activities and interactive experiences, while community organizations and local services connect with residents in meaningful ways. With bike valet available, late-night shopping and dining, and a continually evolving mix of vendors, the market offers something for everyone.

 More than just an event, the Thursday Night Market is a weekly tradition, an opportunity to gather, support local, and celebrate what makes Chico feel like home.

 The market takes place rain or shine (cancelled only if temperatures exceed 110° during market hours). Per CA Health & Safety Code 114259.5, only service animals are permitted.

 NEW: Themed Expos at Thursday Night Market

 This year, the Thursday Night Market is evolving in an exciting new way.

 We're introducing monthly themed expos—dynamic, curated experiences designed to highlight different industries, celebrate community, and bring fresh energy to Downtown Chico throughout the season.

 Hosted in Chico City Plaza, each month will feature a unique theme, creating interactive spaces where visitors can discover, connect, and engage in new ways.

 2026 Expo Lineup:

- April: Health & Wellness Expo

- May: Outdoor Adventures Expo

- June: Unity Through Diversity Expo

- July: Giving Back (Nonprofit Expo)

- August: Wedding & Events Expo

- September: Pet Expo

 April: Health & Wellness Expo

 Sponsored by Healthy Here, a Healthy Communities Collaborative

 Start the season with a focus on feeling your best, inside and out. The Health & Wellness Expo brings together a diverse mix of local businesses and practitioners dedicated to helping people improve their physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing.

 Visitors can explore everything from fitness programs and nutrition guidance to holistic health services, recovery tools, and self-care products. Expect interactive elements like mini demos, wellness tips, and opportunities to connect directly with experts who can support healthier lifestyles.

 Whether you're looking to build better habits, reduce stress, or discover new wellness resources, this expo offers something for everyone.

 May: Outdoor Adventures Expo

 As the weather warms up, the Outdoor Adventures Expo taps into Chico's deep connection to the outdoors. This month celebrates exploration, recreation, and the spirit of adventure.

 Vendors will showcase outdoor gear, guided experiences, travel opportunities, and services that help people get outside and stay active. From hiking and biking to water sports and camping, visitors can discover new ways to experience the region and beyond.

 This expo also highlights environmental stewardship, connecting attendees with organizations and resources focused on protecting and preserving the natural spaces we all enjoy.

 June: Unity Through Diversity Expo

 The Unity Through Diversity Expo is a vibrant celebration of the cultures, traditions, and perspectives that shape our community.

 Throughout the month, visitors will experience a rich blend of cultural expression, through art, music, food, crafts, and storytelling. Local organizations, businesses, and artists will share their heritage and create opportunities for meaningful connection and learning.

 This expo is designed to bring people together, foster understanding, and highlight the beauty of Chico's diversity in an engaging and welcoming environment.

 Unity Through Diversity Vendor Application

 July: Giving Back Expo

 July is all about community impact. The Giving Back Expo shines a spotlight on the nonprofits, service groups, and mission-driven organizations working to make our region stronger, healthier, and more connected.

 Visitors will have the opportunity to meet the people behind these efforts, learn about important causes, and discover ways to get involved—whether through volunteering, donating, or simply spreading awareness.

 From youth programs and education to environmental efforts and social services, this expo highlights the many ways people are making a difference right here in Butte County.

 Giving Back Vendor Application

 August: Wedding & Events Expo

 Celebrate life's biggest moments with the Wedding & Events Expo, a curated showcase of the professionals who bring special occasions to life.

 This expo features local experts across every aspect of event planning, photographers, florists, planners, caterers, venues, entertainers, and more. Visitors can gather ideas, meet potential vendors, and get inspired for weddings, parties, corporate events, and celebrations of all kinds.

 With a focus on creativity and connection, this is the perfect opportunity to discover trusted local talent and start turning event visions into reality.

 Wedding & Events Vendor Application

 September: Pet Expo

 End the season on a high note with a celebration of our furry (and feathered) friends. The Pet Expo brings together pet-focused businesses and organizations dedicated to keeping animals healthy, happy, and thriving.

 Visitors can explore products, services, and resources including pet supplies, training, grooming, veterinary care, and adoption opportunities. It's also a chance to connect with local animal welfare groups and learn more about responsible pet ownership.

 Whether you're a devoted pet parent or simply an animal lover, this expo is a fun, engaging way to connect with Chico's pet-loving community.
