 Taste of Downtown

 Date: Sunday, September 7, 2025

 Time: 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM (Restaurant & Pub Crawl)

 Concert: 3:30 – 5:30 PM at City Plaza (Monkey Wrench)

 Overview

 Get ready to savor the best of Chico! Taste of Downtown returns with a full day of incredible bites, refreshing sips, and live local music throughout the heart of Downtown Chico. With more than 25 restaurants, breweries, and pubs offering exclusive "bites & sips," this is a can't-miss opportunity to sample signature dishes and beverages from Chico's vibrant food and drink scene.

 Attendees can stroll through downtown from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM, enjoying flavorful offerings and live performances from local musicians along the way. The day culminates with a free, family-friendly concert at City Plaza from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM featuring the acclaimed band Monkey Wrench .

 Event Highlights
